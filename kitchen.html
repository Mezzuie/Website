5 Oct 25

I baked a cheesecake!

Recipe:
A healthy amount of oreo crusts
500g Philadelphia cream cheese
140g Sugar
75g Sour Cream
45g Plain Cream
2 eggs
5g vanilla essence

Instructions:
Prepare the oreo inside of a cake mould by adding butter so it sticks
Beat the cream cheese and sugar until smooth for ~8 minutes at 3rd speed
Add sour cream and plain cream bit by bit and beat till smooth
Add eggs one by one and the vanilla essence until mixture becomes fluffy
Pour into mould
Put into oven for ~25 minutes at 180C
